Updates from the StrataProtocol.org team — covering platform releases, methodology refinements, and implementation observations from applying the STRATA Protocol in practice. Published incrementally as the platform and the practitioner community grow.

## What Gets Published Here

**Release notes** — every platform release documented with what shipped, what changed, and the rationale behind the decisions. The [Changelog](/changelog) is the canonical version record; this section provides the narrative context around each release.

**Engineering notes** — observations from building StrataProtocol.org itself: patterns that worked, constraints that shaped decisions, and the self-referential proof of governed delivery at work. The platform builds itself under the framework it documents — that process produces ongoing engineering insight.

**Methodology updates** — refinements to the STRATA Protocol based on application across multiple project types. When a stratum's procedure is updated or a new pattern emerges from practice, it is documented here before propagating to the formal documentation.

**Practitioner stories** — implementation observations from teams applying STRATA on non-reference projects. As the practitioner community grows, this section will reflect the broader ecosystem's experience.
