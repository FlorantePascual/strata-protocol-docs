STRATA Protocol's value is in the methodology — five structured strata that make AI-assisted software delivery governed, traceable, and repeatable. The documentation makes the framework accessible to any practitioner. For those who want to move faster, reduce trial-and-error, or work alongside experienced implementers, we offer direct engagement through a free discovery consultation.

## What We Do

We work directly with founders, technical leads, and engineering teams to implement the STRATA Protocol on their projects. This is not a consulting retainer or a service catalog — it is a practitioner relationship built around a single objective: governed AI-assisted delivery that actually ships.

Engagement forms vary by need. Some practitioners want a coaching relationship for their own implementation work. Others want an advisory presence for a specific initiative. A smaller number engage us as implementation partners on high-stakes commercial builds. In every case, the starting point is the same: a free 20-minute discovery consultation to understand your context, your goals, and how the framework maps to your situation.

## Who This Is For

Direct engagement and qualified access are for practitioners building something with AI — founders, technical leads, engineering managers, and AI builders with active initiatives who want the framework applied to their specific architecture and constraints.

The clearest signals that this is a fit:

- You are building a commercial or enterprise AI-assisted system and want a structured implementation approach
- You are making real architectural and tooling decisions now, and want guidance that is specific to your context
- You want implementation acceleration — not just more documentation to read
- You want to understand exactly how each STRATA stratum applies to what you are building

If you are still at the reading and exploration stage, the public documentation covers the full framework. The consultation and member resources are most valuable once you have an active initiative to apply them to.

## What to Expect from the Discovery Call

The discovery call is 20 minutes, complimentary, and practical from the start.

We will talk through your current initiative, where you are in the implementation lifecycle, and how the STRATA framework applies to your specific context. You will leave the call with a clear picture of how the methodology maps to what you are building — and immediate access to the member resources to act on it.

There is no commitment required and no pitch to sit through. Come ready to talk about what you are building and what kind of support would accelerate your work. The call is productive for anyone who takes it seriously.
