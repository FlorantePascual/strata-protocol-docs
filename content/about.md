Florante Pascual is a seasoned software engineer and technology thought leader with over a decade of experience building enterprise systems, AI-integrated platforms, and developer frameworks. He authored the STRATA Protocol — a governed framework for structured, traceable, and repeatable AI-assisted software delivery — and the Fractal OSI Modeler, an ontological semantic information modeling tool. His work spans a custom ERP for international distributor AMICO Group, the AI9GM governance model, and One Verse Daily, a Bible reading app. Florante specializes in Angular, Vue/Nuxt, and NestJS, and is a practitioner and advocate of governed AI-assisted development.

## About the STRATA Protocol

The STRATA Protocol is a five-stratum governed framework for structured, traceable, and repeatable AI-assisted software delivery. It establishes a clear chain of authority from business requirements through implementation — ensuring every decision is traceable, every deliverable is governed, and every AI-assisted action is aligned with human intent.

The five strata — Classification, Derivation, Authority Chain, Execution Loop, and Artifact Trail — provide development teams with a complete governance model that scales from individual features to enterprise platforms.
